CHENNAI-TAMILNADU

Chennai, formerly Madras, city, capital of Tamil Nadu state, southern India, on the Coromandel Coast of the Bay of Bengal. Known as the “Gateway to South India,” Chennai is a major administrative and cultural centre Chennai is well connected by road, rail, air, and sea. It has an international airport and seaport. Within the city a network of bus services and auto-rickshaws are common modes of transport. The historic town of Mamallapuram with its shore temple, about 37 miles (60 km) south of Chennai, is a popular tourist destination. Chennai is one of the four metropolises in the country. Sited at the Coromandel Coast of the Bay of Bengal, it is today among popular tour places in India visited by large gathering of tourists from all over the world. Marina Beach, St Thomas Mount, Snake Park, Kapaleeshwar Temple, Santhome Cathedral, National Art Gallery, Fort Museum, Birla Planetarium, Government Museum, Valluvar Kottam Memorial, Corporation Complex, Ripon Building, Theosophical Society, Smt Rukmini Devi Arundale Kalakshetra, Guindy National Park, etc are attractions of Chennai city tours in India. Mahabalipuram Group of Monuments and Kanchipuram Temples can be easily reached and visited from Chennai.
